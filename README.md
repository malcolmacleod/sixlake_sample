## sixlake_sample
Data repository to prepare, analyze, and visualize dominant wavelength and turbidity data for six Texas reservoirs. This analysis is complementary to Macleod et al. 2026 "Capturing Spatial Gradients of Water Color and Clarity in Subtropical Reservoirs Under Drought" https://doi.org/10.1029/2025JG009401. The data needed to replicate every analysis in this study is found in Macleod & Powers 2025, a publicly available repository in the Environmental Data Initiative Data Portal https://doi.org/10.6073/pasta/b74eeace199d0a2e3e62dd2c96cf73ed
